The 2% Fallacy

Many investors believe a 2% fee sounds small. "I keep 98%, they take 2%." This is mathematically false. You pay fees on the principal, but you pay them out of the returns.

The Predictive Power of Fees

Data from Morningstar's "Mind the Gap" study reveals a stark truth: The cheaper the fund, the more likely it is to survive and outperform its peers.

Key Takeaway

High-fee funds often rely on "beating the market" to justify their cost. The statistics show they rarely succeed. Low-fee funds simply track the market, but because their hurdle is lower, their net return is consistently higher.
