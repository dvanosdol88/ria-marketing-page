# 04 · Paper & Ink

**Slug:** `paper-and-ink`

**One-liner:** Warm off-white field with dark ink; brand green reserved for the dollar figure and a single keyline under the domain.

A quietly premium concept set on a warm paper field (#FAF8F2) — distinctly NOT mint, more like newsprint or stationery. Dark ink does the typographic work. Brand green appears only twice on the front: on the dollar figure and as a single 2pt keyline under the domain wordmark. The chart is a bar comparison rather than a line — easier to read at a glance and more in keeping with a financial editorial. The back continues the paper field with the same restrained green accent only under the domain, and the indicia clear zone is on a pure-white panel so USPS scanning is unaffected.

**Print-safety verdict: Medium.** Paper-color field requires consistent flood coverage across the whole back to look intentional — a press that drifts on solids will betray banding. Suggest the printer confirm even flood coverage on the off-white.
