## Design commitment

**Loud money.** The `$952,405` lifetime fee gap dominates the front like a
stadium scoreboard. Set in **Archivo Black** at roughly 360px scale, full
brand-green saturation (#00A540), the figure bleeds toward the right trim
edge. Numbers are the visual language; monospace **JetBrains Mono** labels
stamped in caps frame data cards with `001`/`002`/`003` index tags.

- **Ground:** stark deep ink (#0a0a0a) on the front, warm white (#f4f4f0) on
  the back for a hard tonal pivot between sales surface and bio surface.
- **Accent:** single brand green at full saturation for the hero figure,
  legends, and section tags.
- **Red (#D9202F):** strictly reserved for the AUM-loss curve and the
  "Their lifetime fees" stamp — never anywhere else.
- **Layout:** asymmetric, grid-breaking. The hero scoreboard is left-anchored
  and runs nearly full width; stamp cards float right under it; the chart
  occupies the bottom band as an inverted white block on the dark ground.
- **Photo:** David's portrait on the BACK only, in a small 230×230 tight
  square crop, duotone-tinted via CSS (grayscale + sepia + hue-rotate to
  match brand green). The bio block, not the photo, is the focus of the back.
- **Decorative kit:** monospace caption strips, numbered tags, ruled blocks,
  inverted dark CTA card containing the QR.

## Print format

- Trim: 8.5 x 6.25 in landscape, 0.125 in bleed all sides.
- PDF page: 8.75 x 6.5 in. Proof PNGs: 1750 x 1300 px.
- Two pages: 1 = front, 2 = back / mailing side.

## USPS / EDDM compliance

- Top 1/3 of the back (0 → 432 px) reserved for postal use.
- 3px black demarcation rule at y=432.
- Indicia placeholder: PRSRT STD / ECRWSS / U.S. POSTAGE PAID / EDDM RETAIL +
  LOCAL POSTAL CUSTOMER. Printer will validate.
