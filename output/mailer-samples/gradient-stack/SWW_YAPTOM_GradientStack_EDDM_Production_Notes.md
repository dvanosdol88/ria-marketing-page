## Design notes

- BACK: a brand light green (#D6F5E2) gradient fades from 100% on the left edge to
  0% on the right edge, filling the entire area below the separation line. This
  visually weights the left content while keeping the right side clean for the
  large QR.
- BACK upper strip (above separation): light logo on a brand-dark-green plate at
  top-left; USPS EDDM indicia + LOCAL POSTAL CUSTOMER block top-right.
- BACK content (below separation):
  - Center-left: three stacked benefit blocks — Save (a Ton!), Upgrade (your
    Advice), Improve (your Tools) — each header in heavy black with a slightly
    smaller parenthetical sub-phrase, and a single line of detail beneath.
  - Center-right: large 440x440 QR.
  - Bottom-left: DVO portrait + title.
  - Bottom-center: youarepayingtoomuch.com (large bold) over the disclaimer.
- FRONT: domain at top, "$788,000?" headline with footnote, "See screen shot."
  caption with arrow, then the calculator chart card (with input chips and a QR
  overlay on the chart's top-left). Disclaimer at the bottom.
